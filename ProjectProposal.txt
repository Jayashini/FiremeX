C. PROJECT OVERVIEW
C1.  Problem Statement
ℹ  Describe the real-world problem or gap this project addresses. (≈ 100 words)
Traditional safety monitoring methods often depend on human operators to monitor CCTV footage and manually react to abnormal situations. This process is time consuming and is subjected to human errors. It could lead to delayed detection of dangerous events, increasing the risk of injuries, environmental damage, loss of equipment and operational disruptions. 
Standalone sensors can detect changes in the environment, but lack visual context, leading to false alarms; sensors require clear data to meet its high thresholds for confident readings. This accumulated heat alters the sensor's environment and can negatively affect the accuracy of the readings. 

C2.  Proposed Solution & Objectives
ℹ  Summarise the software solution and list 3–5 measurable project objectives.
AI-powered CCTV-based Security Monitoring System that combines computer vision, environmental sensors, real-time risk assessment, automated alerts to fire, improve workplace safety, enhance incident response.
